About Us

"Power up your gaming journey with us!"

Welcome to the Tech Gaming Committee at our college! We are a passionate group of students dedicated to fostering a vibrant gaming community on campus. Our mission is to provide a platform for gamers to connect, compete, and collaborate. From organizing gaming tournaments to hosting workshops on game development, we strive to create an inclusive and engaging environment for all gaming enthusiasts. Join us as we explore the exciting world of technology and gaming together!
